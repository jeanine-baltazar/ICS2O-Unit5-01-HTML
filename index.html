Guess The Random Number

Hello, World! Let's guess the random number. This webpage will generate a random number from 1 to 6 (no decimals). Try to guess the number and type your guess in the "My Guess" input box. Then, click the "Guess" button. If your answer is correct, the webpage will say "Correct!". If not, it will say "Incorrect!". You may guess as many times as you wish by simply changing your answer in the "My Guess" box. Have fun!
